The Pig Game


The point of the Pig game is to try and reach 100 before your opponent. Roll the dice to add to your score, but remember to 'Hold' that score. Holding your score saves that total to your Global score. If you roll a 1 and haven't held your score, it'll be reset to 0, and you'll lose your progress.
